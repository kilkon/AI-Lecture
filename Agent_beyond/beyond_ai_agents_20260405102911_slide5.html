What is Capability Overhang?
Capability overhang is the gap between what AI tools can do and how typical users are using them. This disconnect creates a critical challenge: while AI models have advanced capabilities, most users are only leveraging basic features like writing and translation, missing the full potential of advanced reasoning and delegation.
