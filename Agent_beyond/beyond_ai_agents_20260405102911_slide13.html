Change Management that Works
Six proven practices for successful AI adoption in organizations
Microsoft Keynote
1. Leadership by Example
Executives must model AI usage, remove blockers, and fund platforms to demonstrate commitment
Critical for success
2. Role-based Rollouts
Targeted training for specific roles rather than generic AI education
High impact
3. Champion Networks
Build internal champions who evangelize AI adoption across teams
Essential for scale
4. Behavioral Nudges
Use nudge strategies and best-practice hubs to drive adoption
Effective for engagement
5. Best-practice Hubs
Create centralized resources for sharing AI success stories
Key for knowledge sharing
6. Measurement & Iteration
Track metrics and continuously improve AI implementation
Critical for ROI
Key Insight
Successful AI adoption requires a combination of top-down leadership support and bottom-up champion networks, with role-specific training for maximum impact.
