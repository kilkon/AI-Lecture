Key Targets for 2030
  • Korean AI Foundation Model by ~2030 - Developing proprietary large language models optimized for Korean language and cultural context
  • Domestic NPU Development - National semiconductor strategy for AI chip design and manufacturing
  • AI Talent Pipelines - Training 10,000+ AI specialists through university-industry partnerships
  • AI Infrastructure - Hyperscale computing centers and cloud capabilities
Implication: University-industry-government alignment is critical for achieving these targets, requiring coordinated investment and policy support.
